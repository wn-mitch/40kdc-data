# Faction icon notice

The SVG faction symbols in this directory are fan-made recreations of
Games Workshop faction iconography, taken from the community "Warhammer
Icons Compilation" at https://tcrepo.com/downloads/warhammer-icons-compilation/
(contributors: farvig / BakaDesign, Squirem, Locequen, shitake, runebound,
ExplosiveFlorist). They were renamed to dataset faction ids and run through
svgo (metadata/fill stripping) — the artwork is otherwise unmodified.
`leagues-of-votann.svg` is the compilation's Kharadron Overlords mark used
as a visual stand-in.

These symbols depict marks that are the intellectual property of Games
Workshop Limited. They are used here non-commercially, for a fan-made
